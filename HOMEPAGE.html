Why Save The Earth

With the rising of the costs of living, man has had to come up with ways of developing himself and trying to survive
as the world eveolves. Little did he know that he was the main cause of the problem.
Any opportunity that could take him from poverty to a wealthy life, he embraced with both arms without considering the outcomes, and, sometimes when he knew the consequences, he still ignored and pursued his
selfish goals. Unfortunately, things got out of hand and when he realised, it was almost late.

Saving the earth is now the only solution to mans problem and this can only be done when the earths previous
image is restored. To make it simpler, we mean reaforesting everywhere where man deforested create room for his industries and buildings.
